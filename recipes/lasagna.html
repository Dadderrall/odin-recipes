Garfield's Lasagna

For those that hate Mondays

Grandma showed me how to make this recipe before she slipped on a wet noodle and garfield got so hungry he ate her.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodle
  • Ricotta
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Ground Beef
  • Mozzarella

Directions

  1. Sear the Ground Beef in a skillet
  2. Layer the Noodle on the bottom of a oven dish
  3. Alternate layers of Ground Beef, tomato sauce, ricotta and Noodle until pan is full.
  4. Top the Lasagna with the Mozzarella Cheese
  5. Bake in over for 20 minutes at 369 degrees
