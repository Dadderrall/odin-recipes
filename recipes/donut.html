Homer's Favorite

For those who love D'oh-y goodness

Who wouldn't want the most mouthwatering cartoon treat in TV history?

Ingredients

  • D'oh
  • Frosting
  • Sprinkles

Directions

  1. Knead D'oh into a circle
  2. Bake D'oh for 20 minutes at 420 degrees
  3. Remove from oven and let cool for 10 minutes
  4. Slather frosting on D'oh
  5. Sprinkle liberally
